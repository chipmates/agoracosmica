# Chapters

Six ways to engage with each historical figure: four educational chapters that form a learning arc (receive, engage, connect, demonstrate), plus Free Talk and Council as separate formats outside the arc.

---

## The four chapters

| # | Chapter | What you do | Type |
|---|---------|-------------|------|
| 1 | **Story** | Listen to a ~13-minute biographical episode | Pre-recorded |
| 2 | **Wisdom** | Talk through one teaching with the figure | Live AI |
| 3 | **Prism** | Listen to the figure explore one teaching with three connected thinkers | Pre-recorded |
| 4 | **Quest** | Take a Socratic test of your understanding | Live AI |

Each chapter prepares the next. After Quest, the teaching is "gathered" and shows in your progress.

---

## Outside the arc

| Format | What you do | Type |
|--------|-------------|------|
| **Free Talk** | Open conversation, no agenda | Live AI |
| **Council** | Listen to four figures explore one life question together | Pre-recorded |

---

## What's in the catalog

- **360 stories** · 12 per figure
- **360 wisdom teachings** · 12 per figure
- **360 prism dialogues** · 12 per figure, four figures each
- **110 councils** · 55 questions, two levels each

All audio bilingual English + German with character-level timestamps.
