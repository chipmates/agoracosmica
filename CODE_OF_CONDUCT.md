# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

Agora Cosmica is a project by ChipMates gemeinnützige GmbH, a German nonprofit. We are building a space where philosophical wisdom is accessible to everyone. This extends to how we work together as a community.

We are committed to making participation in this project a respectful, constructive, and welcoming experience for everyone, regardless of age, ability, ethnicity, gender identity, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual orientation.

---

## Expected Behavior

- **Be respectful.** Treat others the way you want to be treated. Disagreement is fine, disrespect is not.
- **Be constructive.** Offer helpful feedback. Focus on the work, not the person.
- **Be welcoming.** Help newcomers get started. Answer questions patiently. Remember that everyone was new once.
- **Be professional.** Keep discussions focused on the project. Assume good intent.
- **Be inclusive.** Use language that is welcoming to a broad audience. Respect differing viewpoints and experiences.

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Personal attacks, insults, or deliberately inflammatory comments
- Unwelcome or inappropriate contact of any kind
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Sustained disruption of discussions or contributions
- Any conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces: GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, code reviews, and any other communication channel associated with Agora Cosmica. It also applies when representing the project in public spaces.

---

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project team at **agoracosmica@chipmates.ai**. All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

The project team will determine the appropriate response, which may include:

1. **Correction.** A private conversation about the impact of the behavior.
2. **Warning.** A formal notice with expectations for future conduct.
3. **Temporary restriction.** A temporary ban from project spaces.
4. **Permanent restriction.** A permanent ban from project spaces.

All reports will be handled with discretion and respect for the privacy of everyone involved.
